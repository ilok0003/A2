Influenza Visualization

This visualization offers a comprehensive understanding of the influenza cases spread across different regions. It sheds light on the patterns, intensities, and fluctuations in cases, ensuring a better grasp on the disease's impact. By analyzing the data, one can identify potential hotspots, the effectiveness of preventive measures, and the overall toll on communities. This aids in formulating informed strategies and public health policies.

Scatter Plot Analysis

This scatter plot visualizes the relationship between the total number of deaths, total cases, and the overall population for influenza cases per country in 2017. Through this, one can discern patterns and correlations, providing valuable insights into the severity and spread of influenza in various nations.

Choropleth Map Overview

The choropleth map displays influenza cases per 10,000 population, offering a geographical perspective on the disease's prevalence. This visualization allows for an easy comparison between regions, highlighting areas with a higher incidence of cases and those relatively unaffected.
