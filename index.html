Influenza Visualization

This visualization provides a comprehensive view of influenza cases across regions. It delineates patterns, intensities, and case fluctuations, facilitating a deeper understanding of the disease's ramifications. By examining this data, users can pinpoint hotspots, measure the success of preventive actions, and gauge the overall community impact, thereby guiding public health policy decisions.

Scatter Plot Analysis

This scatter plot presents the correlation among total deaths, total cases, and the overall population for influenza cases by country in 2017. This representation helps identify patterns and correlations, shedding light on the disease's severity and distribution across nations.

Choropleth Map Overview

The choropleth map illustrates the influenza cases per 10,000 population, granting a geographic insight into the disease's prevalence. This map facilitates easy regional comparisons, underscoring areas with higher case densities and those that are relatively untouched.
